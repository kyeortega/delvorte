It's Midnight and I'm Watching You Drive
I am sitting in your Toyota,
as the illuminated city passes by.
Each light beams into the car,
hoping to steal my attention.

I let my eyes follow their rushing dance,
their swift steps that skim your skin,
tracing figures up a tender canvas,
to end the choreography in your eyes.

Your hands grip tight to the wheel,
yet exude a softness so endearing,
only reaching the height of its bliss
within my wistful imagination.

While evoking a melodic question,
harmoniously dissolving silence,
your sweet notes flush my ears,
both soothing and searing them.

My stomach churns violent waves,
stinging walls with every crash,
and my heart tumbles and thrashes
in an attempt to escape the fever.

But in the moment your eyes find mine,
under gifted glow of red light,
there is strange comfort knowing that
you are my new beginning.

I love you,
Belle :)
